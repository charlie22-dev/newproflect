The Mimalicious Manifesto

More Than
Just Meat

Mimalicious isn't just a burger joint—it's a revolution against boring food. We started with a simple question: "Why settle for a burger that looks like it gave up on life?" We’re here to give your tastebuds a wake-up call with sizzle, spice, and stacks that defy gravity.

The Mima Commandments

We live by a strict code of burger-conduct to ensure every bite is legendary:

  • The Golden Ratio: Every patty must be juice-to-bun perfect. No dry bites allowed.
  • Fresh Over Frozen: If it's been in a freezer longer than a polar bear, we don't serve it.
  • The Cheese Rule: If it doesn't stretch, it's not doing its job.
  • Zero Regrets: Eat it like you mean it. We don't do small portions here.
